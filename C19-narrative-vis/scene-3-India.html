India: Stringency Index and Covid-19 New Cases

In India, we observe drastic response to COVID-19 outbreak, as seen by the steep increase in stringency index to almost full lockdown or 100 in the index scale. However, new COVID-19 cases is increasing at nearly exponential level over the course of end of March until end of June, with almost 20,000 cases recorded daily.
