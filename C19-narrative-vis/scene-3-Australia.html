Australia: Stringency Index and Covid-19 New Cases

First, we will observe data from Australia. From the data we observe that around mid of March, new COVID-19 increasing sharply, followed by drastic increase in stringency index. By April, we observe that new cases has decreased. Australia then gradually lowered down the lockdown measures as indicated by gradual decrease in stringency index. However on end of June, there are increase in COVID-19 cases observed.
