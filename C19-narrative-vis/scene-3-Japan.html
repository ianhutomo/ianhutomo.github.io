Japan: Stringency Index and Covid-19 New Cases

Japan is a case where there were no drastic lockdown measures applied. While we still see gradual increase in stringency index on February until early March, Japan stringency index was never going over 50 at the scale index. We also observe a gradual decrease in the index by mid of May.

The new cases appears to be increasing significantly over the course of April, and seems to be significantly decreased by May. We also observe a slight increase in daily cases in June after the stringency index lowered to around 30 on the scale.
